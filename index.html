Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi, India that was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in 1639. It is one of the most iconic landmarks of India and is known for its red sandstone walls and intricate carvings.

Konark Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple is a historic temple located in Odisha, India. It is one of the most iconic landmarks of India and is known for its unique design and intricate carvings.

Charminar

The Charminar is a historic monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the most iconic landmarks of India and is known for its unique design and intricate carvings.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple is a historic temple located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is one of the most iconic landmarks of India and is known for its unique design and intricate carvings.

Lakshmi Vilas Palace

The Lakshmi Vilas Palace is a historic palace located in Vadodara, Gujarat, India. It is one of the most iconic landmarks of India and is known for its unique design and intricate carvings.
